The 3-Question Framework

1. WHEN? (Time Zone)

Is the action in the Past, Present, or Future?

2. WHAT TYPE? (Aspect)
  • Simple: Just the fact / routine.
  • Continuous: In progress / temporary.
  • Perfect: Already done / connection to another time.
3. SIGNAL WORDS? (Clues)

Look for triggers like "yesterday", "since", "by tomorrow".

The "STOP & THINK" Method: Pause, check the 3 questions, pick your tense, GO!

Quick Decision Flowchart

PAST

  • Completed? → Simple Past
  • Still going then? → Past Cont.
  • Happened before another past? → Past Perfect

PRESENT

  • Habit/Fact? → Simple Present
  • Happening NOW? → Present Cont.
  • Connected to present? → Present Perfect

FUTURE

  • Will it happen? → Simple Future
  • Will be in progress? → Future Cont.
  • Completed before a future time? → Future Perfect
Golden Rule: Time + Aspect = Tense
